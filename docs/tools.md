## AI 辅助

| 用法 | 提示词 |
| --- | --- |

| 练口语 | `Act as my English conversation partner. Ask one question at a time and correct my answer naturally.` |
| 生成例句 | `Give me 5 natural example sentences for the phrase "...", from easy to advanced.` |
| 精读文章 | `Explain this paragraph in simple English, then list useful phrases.` |
